CANBERRA'S ART BIENNIAL

Contour 556 returns for the 4th edition in October 2022.


Canberra’s Art Biennial comprises core installations around the central lake landscape (contour 556), as well as an expanded presence in Canberra City/City Hill. Artists will be responding to the designed city of Canberra: represented by Australia’s political, cultural and physical history. We have consolidated our support with locals and others around Australia through a successful fundraising event concluded in June 2021. The event engages people of all ages and abilities, delighting, puzzling and encouraging interaction with art and public space.
